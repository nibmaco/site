Who We Are?

Nimbaco is a consulting company located in the East Bay, Antioch, CA. We provide application security and infrastructure support, custom software development and consulting solutions for small to large businesses using industry standard, program and project management best practices. Our focus is to provide a solution for our clients with an emphasis on end results that will enhance your competitive edge, regardless of size, location, industry or budget. We work with clients to identify their requirements, then using project and program management best practices, we work to develop and build a scaleable and reliable solution within budget and time constraints. Our philosophy is to prioritize our client’s needs and respond with a sense of urgency, courtesy, respect and professionalism at all times. In today’s changing technological landscape, we believe in the continuous update of the skills of all of our developers and staff so that we keep pace with changes across all industries and stay a step ahead of the on-going technology changes.

What Do We Do?

Our development team is skilled at developing solutions using any modern programming language and we are passionate about staying ahead of the technology curve and utilizing the latest tools. We push the technology envelope every day. We are platform agnostic, meaning we work with virtually any technology stack, from Java to .NET and everything in between. No matter the platform or programming language, we can develop to your specific needs and we even have our own code library to speed things up. Our pre-built code library, detailed specs illustrated design method, and quick development process make it possible for us to build software solutions quickly. So no matter how far out your idea seems; be it a cloud infrastructure support, architecture or design, a quirky consumer application or complex business software, we provide the design, development, technology, and methodology to bring it to life. We are able to scale your business software solutions as large or small as you require both horizontally and vertically. If there is something specific you are looking for but don’t see it listed, give us a call; we are confident we can build the software solution that’s right for you! Our development team is the best in the industry.
