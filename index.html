Nimbaco,Inc

The data platform for all.

NIMBACO is a software as a service company offering a self service data platform with analysis and crm functionality built in. CRM is a critical new tool for today's businesses. NIMBACO takes it to the next level by offering you the ability to bring your data analysis back in-house. You will have the tools to analyze your data to maximize your profits and we've taken the "technical" out of technology making it easy enough for anyone to operate. Whether you're a small business who feels priced out of the new crm market or a large company who wants to efficiently organize your own marketing campaigns, NIMBACO is the partner for you! Our goal is to put control of your data back in your hands, knowing that you are best situated to manage and utilize it.

NIMBACO's self service data platform ensures that you, the platform user, control how and when you utilize your data by enabling your business, regardless of size, to manipulate your data allowing you to automatically generate reports and dashboards and provide personalized, targeted ads to your customers. You won't even need an IT engineer to help you configure or generate your campaign or analytics, our platform is simple enough for anyone to use.
